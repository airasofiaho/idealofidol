DETAILS
In Mnet's survival show, Produce 101, 101 female trainees from different entertainment labels in South Korea compete for eleven spots for the national group, I.O.I. From the very first evaluation to the final verdict, only eleven girls survived and got their spots, a feeling of uncertainty already relieved as they get their opportunities to pursue their dreams as artists in which they are trained for. Despite of the ten-month limit of promoting, I.O.I has left its mark on many KPOP fans alike, awaiting a reunion in five years time.
